# Privacy Policy

**App Name:** Last Updated  
**Effective Date:** 18 April 2026  

This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

## 1. Data Collection  
The App does **not collect, store, or process personal data** from users or visitors.

## 2. External Requests  
The App makes a request to the website’s publicly available sitemap (`/sitemap.xml`) to retrieve update information. This data is already publicly accessible and is not stored by the App.

## 3. Data Storage  
No personal or site data is stored, shared, or sold.

## 4. Third-Party Services  
The App operates within the Wix platform and follows its security and privacy standards.

## 5. Changes to this Policy  
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App implies acceptance of any changes.
